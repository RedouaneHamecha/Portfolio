About Me

Bridging Infrastructure & Intelligence

I am transitioning into data analysis, a field where I am just getting started with a lot of enthusiasm.
What sets me apart is my background in IT Infrastructure, namely my experience as a networks and systems technician, which gives me a different perspective on data.
